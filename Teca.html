Teca

Book design

2022


A transversal narrative on the Ducal Palace of Urbino that emerges from the photographic selection curated by Armin Linke, in collaboration with the MA Photography course of ISIA Urbino. The narrative is developed around the theme of display cases, in a broad sense. Focusing on display systems, custody and confinement, the publication tries to go through the history of the Palace starting from the Theft of the Century in ‘75, taking as an example two great cases in the history of Italian architecture, Carlo Scarpa and the BBPR Group.
