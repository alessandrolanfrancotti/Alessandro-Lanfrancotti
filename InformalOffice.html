Informal Office

Book design and Brand identity

2023


Informal Research is a tool provided by Informal Office, which aims to explore the topic of collaboration in the field of Graphic Design and to analyse the different types of existing realities, such as Publishing Houses and Exhibition Spaces, which can be initiated and applied in the context of contemporary Graphic Design.

Informal Office is a Graphic Design studio specialising in book design, brand identity, web design and exhibition design. The studio is a project born from a collaboration between three graphic designers, offering an art direction and consultancy service. Comparison, experimentation and collaboration with other realities are the main values of the collective.
